List of Colleges in Delhi University: Affiliated/ Recognised Colleges in DU

Delhi University is India's most prestigious, significant, and sought-after higher education institution. Below is an up-to-date list of Delhi University colleges that are affiliated to which students can apply.

Delhi University is the best known for providing students with enriching educational opportunities that can help them seize favourable opportunities in their particular career paths. For their educational desires, many 10+2 students and recent graduates are eager to apply for admission to DU Affiliated Colleges and Universities. Every year, more than 2 lakh applications are submitted to the Delhi University. University of Delhi which is popularly known as DU has a total of 77 constituent DU colleges and 5 other recognized institutes that enjoy support from the nodal body. These DU colleges are categorized as Delhi University North Campus colleges and Delhi University South Campus colleges.

Delhi University is a highly esteemed academic institution that comprises numerous esteemed institutions offering a wide array of academic programmes. Among these, St. Stephen's College is notable for its dedication to a holistic education. Lady Shri Ram College for Women is a frequently ranked top institution that provides unmatched chances for women's academic accomplishment and development. Hindu College is a centre for innovative study and its vibrant faculty and rich history. Renowned for its caring atmosphere, Miranda House cultivates a strong sense of community and intellectual curiosity. These establishments, in addition to others such as Kirori Mal College, Shri Ram College of Commerce, and others, all help to make Delhi University known as a centre for its academic excellence and reputation.

Courses in Engineering, Medicine, Science, and other fields are available at DU Affiliated Colleges. The admissions process for Medical and Engineering varies, though the complete guide to BA, BSc and BCom admissions can be found here. Additionally, some colleges at Delhi University, such as Jesus and Mary College and St. Stephen's College, have their own admissions procedures and eligibility requirements for the courses they offer. Based on the revised DU admissions process, admission to Delhi University colleges is based either on merit or on performance in the CUET entrance exam.

It's interesting to note that DU colleges also offer ECA quota and Sports quota admissions, for which applicants must present proof of participation and participate in trials before being accepted. At Delhi University, academic achievement is important, but talent and how it is valued are equally important. Some of the best actors, directors, businessmen, CEOs, singers, athletes, etc. have all graduated from Delhi University colleges (DU Colleges) in the past. This is true of both Delhi University South Campus Colleges and Delhi University North Campus Colleges.

DU Admissions 2024

DU admissions process for UG and PG courses at various DU Colleges has been revised. For UG courses, Delhi University will now have a common entrance exam. Admission to DU will be conducted by CUET Exam. The questions to be asked in these CUET entrance exams are based on the topics included in the CBSE Board Class 12 Syllabus. The competition for these entrance exams are also very high. So, students must go through the CBSE Board Class 12 Previous Year Question Papers to prepare for the same. For admission to UG courses at Delhi University, students must first clear the CUET entrance exam and then apply for CSAS counselling for final admission.

Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS) will be used to determine admission to all DU-affiliated colleges based on CUET scores. There will be three stages to the admissions procedure. Candidates must complete the CSAS 2024 application form in phase 1. Students must choose their preferred course and college during the second phase. Seat distribution over several rounds and final admission based on a merit list will be part of the third phase.

Earlier, admission to a few UG and all PG courses was given through DU-conducted entrance exams, namely the Delhi University Entrance Test (DUET) or the Delhi University LLB Entrance Exam followed by a Personal Interview (PI). However, from 2024, DU will offer PG courses through CUET PG.

Delhi University Seat 2024

Delhi University has approximately 69,554 undergraduate seats available across its colleges. Both categories of DU 2024 seats are regular seats and supernumerary seats. In order to calculate their chances of admission to their preferred course, candidates must understand how DU seats will be distributed among colleges and courses in 2024. It should be noted that the ECA and sports quotas reserve 5% of all available seats at all colleges affiliated with Delhi University.

FAQ
How can I apply for admission to St.StephensCollege/ Jesus and Mary College at DU?

These colleges have their own registration forms which require the applicant to fill in the registration number of their University of Delhi registration. So the applicant should first fill out the common form on the admission portal on the website, then visit the website of these colleges and wait for notifications regarding their registration process. Further process of registration will happen on their website.

What should be the validity of my OBC certificate while applying for DU Colleges?

The applicant applying under any category should have the caste certificate (in this case OBC non-creamy layer certificate) of the current financial year, that is issued on or after 31st March 2022.

Do DU Colleges provide admission in B.Sc.(H) Biotechnology?

No, the University of Delhi does not provide admission in B.Sc. Biotechnology but the subject Biotechnology is used to determine eligibility criteria for various B.Sc. courses.

Do DU Colleges provide reservations to Jain applicants?

No, there is no reservation for Jain applicants.

If I do not meet the first cutoff of DU Colleges, then will I have to apply again for second cutoffs and so on?

No, you do not need to apply again for the second cutoff. However, you need to apply again for the course or college you want in a particular cutoff if you meet the cutoff."

How can I apply for admission to B.Ed., LLB, and Bachelor of Library and Information Science at DU colleges?

As these are master's courses, you can apply for admission to these courses in the PG admission portal on the University of Delhi website du.ac.in.

Do DU Colleges provide any reservation for students who are residents of Delhi?

No, DU colleges do not provide any such reservation to the residents of Delhi.

Your 12th result has not been declared yet so should I fill the registration form for DU College Admission now or wait for the result?

You need to register yourself on the UG Admission Portal and select nbsp;the results awaited. Once your result is declared then you can upload your marks on the admission portal but registration is compulsory right now.

Can I self-attest my mark sheet using Digi Locker app for DU colleges?

Yes, you can self-attest your mark-sheet using Digi Locker app.

Is there a single form for admission to UG merit and entrance-based courses at DU Colleges?

Yes, the candidates can register for both merit-based and entrance-based courses using a single online registration form. In the entrance tab if the applicant selects YES for entrance-based courses then the applicant is applying for both merit based and selected entrance-based courses. If the applicant selects NO in the entrance tab then the applicant only applies for all the merit-based courses.

How many colleges are associated to Delhi University?

There are 91 colleges that are associated with Delhi University in total, 64 of which offer CSAS-based admissions.

Delhi University has how many campuses?

There are six campuses in Delhi University: North Campus, West Campus, Dhaula Kuan Campus, South Campus, East Campus, and Central Campus.

How many undergraduate seats are available for admission at DU Colleges?

As of 2022, there are 69,554 seats available in Delhi University colleges across various courses.

What is the most typical process of admission to undergraduate programmes at Delhi University-affiliated colleges?

he merit achieved by candidates in the CUET UG 2022 examination is the primary mode of admission to undergraduate courses at Delhi University affiliated colleges.

Does DU Colleges have any undergraduate programmes that require an entrance exam?

From the academic year 2022 & 2023, NTA will be hosting the CUET, which will offer admission to undergraduate programmes at DU.
